Braid Mobile gets friendlier this release. Leave the app in your pocket for a while and it now wakes up and reconnects on its own, no more staring at a frozen screen. You'll also get a nudge when your agent needs you even if the app is closed, plus a plain terminal tab for when you just want a quick shell.

{/* truncate */}

## Added

- **Get notified, even when the app is closed** - Braid Mobile can now ping you when an agent finishes or needs your attention, even if the app isn't open. Want to keep things quiet? There's a separate toggle to turn these off while still getting alerts when the app is open.
- **A plain terminal tab** - Sometimes you just want a shell. The new-tab menu now lets you open a simple terminal with no agent attached.

## Improved

- **Reconnects on its own** - Come back to the app after a while and it quietly reconnects, showing a "connecting" screen instead of getting stuck. Your home screen also shows which worktree each session belongs to, and Settings now shows your app version.
- **Up-to-date usage numbers** - Pull to refresh and your Claude and Codex usage actually updates now.

## Fixed

- **Cleaner notifications** - Removing a device now fully disconnects it, so you won't keep getting alerts for phones or desktops you've already let go.
